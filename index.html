About Us

Independence is
our greatest asset.

Unify Investment Group is a global principal investment and strategic advisory conglomerate, jointly established by several internationally renowned family offices, managing a proprietary capital pool exceeding US$700 million.

Unlike traditional asset managers, we allocate strictly our own capital and manage no outside public funds. This independence keeps us immune to short-term market pressures, upholds true long-termism, and builds profound strategic partnerships with our portfolio companies.

Our vision is built on rigorous data science: one of the world's top four patent databases and advanced AI technologies power comprehensive technical reviews, market intelligence and competitor analysis — seeing through complexity to identify ideal opportunities well ahead of the market.

VISION

Inheriting the generational wealth wisdom of prominent family offices to discover future industry leaders with a cross-cycle perspective.

AGILITY

Operating entirely with proprietary capital preserves exceptional funding flexibility — tailoring the most compliant, suitable capital solutions for each enterprise.
